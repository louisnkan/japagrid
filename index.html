What this is

JapaGrid is not a blog. It's not a listicle farm. It's a single person's research notebook, published. Every country profile follows the same structure — visa pathways, job market reality, cost versus earnings, qualification recognition, lifestyle and integration, and the catch no one puts in the headline. Every claim links to an official source. Every page is date-stamped. If the data is stale, that's on me, and I'll say so.

I'm not an immigration lawyer. I'm not a consultant. I'm someone doing the same research you're doing, with the advantage of having spent too many hours on it already. Take what I write as a starting point, not gospel. Verify everything yourself. But at least you'll know what to verify.
